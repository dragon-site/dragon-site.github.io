DRAGON’S REMITTANCE REACH

Dragon is striving for a win win, value-add in every engagement and believe in giving the power back to people and businesses, to take control of their own digital finances while keeping costs low, speed high, secure and convenient - all in the palm of your hand.

E-GAMING

Dragon joins the fast growing industry
DRAGON’S E-GAMING REACH

E-Gaming is one of the fastest growing industries with an ever increasing number of people across the globe participating in gaming or watching live streams over the internet. With the global games market predicted to exceed a colossal $138 billion in 2019, it is rapidly evolving into one of the hottest and most profitable entertainment industries. With Dragon, gamers around the globe will have virtually instant purchasing power at their fingertips. Whether it’s access to E-Gaming events, to getting hold of the latest merchandise, or in game purchases like providing their character with new skins. Dragon Coin, the world’s number one entertainment coin, will make this a seamless, user-friendly experience.
