PAUL MOYNAN
International Chief Executive Officer

Paul Moynan is a dynamic and experienced leader with decades of success gained through working in a diverse theatre of operations, crossing over from military to government and then into corporate management on the global stage.

Paul has a unique skill set gained from serving as a Royal Marines Commando, working with several High Net Worth families across the Middle East and as an advisor to the Offices of President of African States.

A leading technologist, Paul has helped grow Dragon Coin into the world’s most widely used entertainment coin and is always looking for easier, better and more efficient ways to solve problems using cutting edge Blockchain technology.

Paul is an enthusiastic supporter of socially conscious ventures and is able to bring a wealth business acumen and top-level experience to any project. This has lead to the development of an impressive portfolio of industry leading companies ranging from Art, Blockchain, E-Gaming, Gaming, Hospitality, Motor Sports and professional athletes.

Paul brings a wealth of top-level expertise to Dragon and is the driving force behind the evolution of remittance systems for the 21st Century.

In Paul’s words, ‘Dragon is not about building a platform - we are creating an entrire ecosystem.’
